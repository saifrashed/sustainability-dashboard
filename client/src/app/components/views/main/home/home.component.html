About GO-HvA

re-set (towards sustainable through and through) is a three-year strategic program to give the AUAS a powerful impulse in its development into a leading sustainable university of applied sciences. The program runs from 2019 to 2021 and focuses on making business operations, research and education more sustainable. At the end of the program, the mission, vision, objectives and the resulting activities of re-set have landed in the DNA of the Hogeschool van Amsterdam.

In general, the AUAS's organizational units are working on sustainable change. The program has a vibrant network of change agents in the faculties and services, who are pivotal in the work within the organizational units. Through meetups, events, an online platform and a strong community, this network of change runs right through the AUAS.
